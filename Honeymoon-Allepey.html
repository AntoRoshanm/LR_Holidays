Overview

Officially called Alappuzha, Alleppey is known for its network of canals and lagoons with beautiful backwaters and the houseboats offering overnight stays. Located in the southern part of Kerala, the coastline of Alleppey offers some of the best beaches with water sports during the dry season.

FAQs about Allepey HoneyMoon

What is the best time to Honeeymoon Visit in Allepey?

Ultimately, the best time for your honeymoon visit to Alleppey depends on your preferences for weather, activities, and crowd levels. Whether you choose to visit during the peak season to enjoy pleasant weather or during the monsoon to experience the romantic allure of the rains, Alleppey offers a unique and memorable honeymoon experience throughout the year.

How many minimum days are required to explore Allepey ?

To explore Alleppey (Alappuzha) thoroughly and enjoy its backwaters, beaches, and cultural attractions, it's recommended to spend at least 2 to 3 days in the region. This duration allows you to cover the major highlights and experience the essence of Alleppey without rushing.

How safe is houseboat stay

How to reach Allepey?

By Air: The nearest airport to Alleppey is Cochin International Airport (COK), located approximately 75 kilometers away. From the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Alleppey. The journey by road takes approximately 2 to 3 hours, depending on traffic and road conditions. Cochin International Airport is well-connected to major cities in India and abroad, making it a convenient option for travelers.

By Train: Alleppey has its own railway station, Alleppey Railway Station (also known as Alappuzha Railway Station), which is well-connected to major cities in India, including Chennai, Bangalore, Mumbai, and Delhi. You can reach Alleppey by train and then hire a taxi or take an auto-rickshaw to your desired destination within the town. The railway station is located in the heart of the city, making it a convenient option for travelers arriving by train.

By Road: Alleppey is well-connected by road to major cities and towns in Kerala and neighboring states. You can reach Alleppey by private car, taxi, or bus. The most common routes to reach Alleppey are: From Kochi (Cochin): Take NH66 via Cherthala to reach Alleppey. From Trivandrum: Take NH66 via Kollam and Kayamkulam to reach Alleppey. From Bangalore: Take NH44 via Salem, Coimbatore, and Thrissur to reach Alleppey. The journey duration varies depending on the starting point, but it typically takes around 4 to 6 hours from nearby cities.

By Bus: Kerala State Road Transport Corporation (KSRTC) operates regular bus services to Alleppey from cities like Kochi, Trivandrum, and Bangalore. Private bus operators also offer services to Alleppey from various cities. You can find both regular and deluxe buses depending on your comfort preference. Additionally, there are interstate bus services connecting Alleppey to neighboring states like Tamil Nadu and Karnataka.
