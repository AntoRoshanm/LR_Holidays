FAQs about Murudeshwar Bachelor

What is the best time to Visit in Murudeshwar?

Winter season is considered as the best season to visit this serene town as the weather is perfect for a relaxing vacation. You can visit this town between October and March to make the most out of your trip. If you want to attend the grand celebrations of Maha Shivratri at Murudeshwar Temple then you should plan your visit in February or March.

How many Days are Enough to Visit Murudeshwar?

You need at least 2 days for Murudeshwar.

Is Murudeshwar safe for Bachelors?

Yes, Murudeshwar is quite safe for everyone.

How to reach Murudeshwar?

Murudeshwar has its own railway station, Murudeshwar Railway Station (MRDW), which is well-connected to major cities like Bangalore, Mangalore, and Mumbai. Several trains operate to and from Murudeshwar on a daily basis. From the railway station, you can easily reach your accommodation or any other destination in Murudeshwar by auto-rickshaw or taxi.
