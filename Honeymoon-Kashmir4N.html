Overview

Kashmir is the northernmost geographical region of the Indian subcontinent. Until the mid-19th century, the term "Kashmir" denoted only the Kashmir Valley between the Great Himalayas and the Pir Panjal Range. Today, the term encompasses a larger area that includes the India-administered territories of Jammu and Kashmir and Ladakh.

Which month is best for Kashmir

The best time to visit Kashmir is from March to September. When spring arrives in late March, it brings with it blue skies, seasonal blooms and green meadows perfect for picnics. Summer, from May to September, sees mild temperatures, clear views and large crowds.

How to reach Kashmir?

By air -Several airlines operate regular flights to Srinagar, making it accessible from different parts of the country. The airport is located approximately 12 kilometers from the city center of Srinagar.

The most convenient way to reach Kashmir is by air. Srinagar International Airport, also known as Sheikh ul-Alam Airport, is the main airport serving the region. It is well-connected to major cities in India such as Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, and Kolkata, as well as to some international destinations.

By train -Jammu Tawi Railway Station, located in the city of Jammu, is the nearest major railway station to Kashmir. It is well-connected to major cities in India such as Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, and Chennai.

From Jammu, you can continue your journey to Kashmir by road. There are regular bus services and taxis available from Jammu to Srinagar, covering a distance of approximately 270 kilometers.
