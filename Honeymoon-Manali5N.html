Overview

Manali is a town, near Kullu town in Kullu district in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh.It is situated in the northern end of the Kullu Valley, formed by the Beas River. The town is located in the Kullu district, approximately 270 kilometres (170 mi) north of the state capital of Shimla and 544 kilometres (338 mi) northeast of the national capital of New Delhi.

FAQs about Manali HoneyMoon

What is the best time to Honeeymoon Visit in Manali?

The best time to visit Manali is from December to March.

Which month is best for Manali

The best time to visit Manali is generally from March to June for pleasant weather and various outdoor activities. September to November is also a good time for fewer crowds and clear skies.

Which are the famous places in Manali

Some of the most famous tourist places in Manali are Hadimba Devi Temple,Solang Valley,Rohtang Pass,Manu Temple,Old Manali,Nehru Kund,Beas River,Vashisht Hot Water Springs and Temple

Is Manali safe for HoneyMoon Couples?

Yes, Manali is quite safe for everyone.

How to reach Manali?

By air -The nearest airport to Manali is Bhuntar Airport (Kullu-Manali Airport), located in Bhuntar town, approximately 50 kilometers south of Manali. The airport is well-connected to major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Chandigarh, with regular flights operated by various airlines.

From Bhuntar Airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Manali, which usually takes around 1.5 to 2 hours depending on road conditions.

By train -The nearest major railway station to Manali is Chandigarh Railway Station, located approximately 310 kilometers away. From Chandigarh, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Manali.

Alternatively, you can travel by train to Pathankot Railway Station, which is around 290 kilometers from Manali. From Pathankot, you can continue your journey by road via NH154.
